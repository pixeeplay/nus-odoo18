Your customers pay at their own rhythm. Your sales amplify.

Configuration

Go to the 'Invoice/Configuration/provider' menu and select Alma.
Set your test and production Alma api key in the credentials tab and the desired options in the Configuration tab.
Don't forget the linked payment journal (default Bank), the Alma payment method and publish the provider.


Refund available

You can easily refund the payment completely or partially. To do this, go to payment, click on REFUND and enter the amount to be refunded
